Getting Started in Evony

Evony: The King's Return is a complex strategy game where you build your city, train troops, and compete with other players. Here's what every new player needs to know:

Quick Start Tips

  • Complete the tutorial - it gives great rewards
  • Join an alliance as soon as possible
  • Always keep your builders busy
  • Don't neglect your wall defenses
  • Use a peace shield when offline

Understanding the Basics

Evony has several key components you'll need to master:

Your Keep

The center of your empire. Upgrade this first as it unlocks other buildings and higher levels.

Troops

Four types: Infantry, Cavalry, Archers, and Siege. Each counters another type in battle.

Generals

Lead your armies and provide buffs. Some are better for attacking, others for defense.

First 7 Days Strategy

The first week is crucial for setting up your long-term success. Follow this progression:

Day 1: Complete Tutorial

Follow the tutorial completely. It will guide you through basic buildings, training troops, and attacking monsters.

Day 2-3: Resource Production

Focus on building and upgrading your resource production buildings (Farms, Sawmills, Quarries, Iron Mines).

Day 4: Join an Alliance

Find an active alliance that fits your playstyle. Alliances provide protection, resources, and help.

Day 5-6: Military Buildings

Build up your barracks, archery range, stable, and workshop. Start training a balanced army.

Day 7: First PvE Content

Start attacking lower level monsters and joining alliance rallies on bigger monsters.

Important!

The game gives new players a 7-day beginner's bubble. Do not let this expire without having another bubble ready! You will be vulnerable to attacks.

Resource Management

Resources are the lifeblood of your empire. You'll need four main types:

Food
  • Used to train troops
  • Consumed by troops daily
  • Prioritize early game
Wood
  • Used for most buildings
  • Needed for research
  • Important all game
Stone
  • Used for defensive buildings
  • Needed for wall upgrades
  • Mid-game priority
Iron
  • Used for high-level troops
  • Needed for siege equipment
  • Late-game priority

Resource Gathering Tips

  • Upgrade resource tiles outside your city regularly
  • Send troops to gather from world resource tiles
  • Complete daily quests for resource rewards
  • Join alliance resource tiles for bonus gathering speed
  • Use resource items from your inventory when needed

Building Strategy

Buildings are the foundation of your city. Upgrade them in this priority order:

Building Priority Reason
Keep Highest Unlocks other buildings
Academy High Research provides permanent buffs
Resource Buildings High Steady resource flow
Military Buildings Medium Stronger troops for defense/attack
Wall Medium Defense against attacks

Building Tips

  • Always have buildings upgrading - use speedups during events
  • Upgrade resource storage before large upgrades
  • Plan your city layout with defense in mind
  • Don't neglect your hospital - it saves wounded troops

Alliance Guide

Joining an alliance is the single most important thing you can do in Evony:

Protection

Alliance members can reinforce you when attacked and provide territory protection.

Resources

Get help with construction and receive alliance gifts from purchases.

Teamwork

Participate in rallies against powerful monsters and enemy players.

Choosing an Alliance

Look for these qualities in a good alliance:

  • Active members (50+ online daily)
  • Helpful leadership (R4/R5 who answer questions)
  • Similar timezone (For coordinated events)
  • Clear rules (About participation expectations)

Daily Routine

An efficient daily routine will maximize your progress:

Activity Frequency Priority
Collect Resources Every 2-4 hours High
Complete Daily Quests Daily High
Attack Monsters 3-5 times/day Medium
Gather Resources Continuous Medium
Check Mail/Alliance Chat Multiple times/day High

Weekly Activities

  • Server War (SvS) - Every 2 weeks (participate for rewards)
  • Kill Event (KE) - When active (focus on monster killing)
  • Alliance Fest - Weekly event (complete tasks for rewards)

Common Beginner Mistakes

Avoid these common pitfalls that new players make:

1. Not Using a Shield

Going offline without a peace shield is the #1 way to lose resources and troops.

2. Training Wrong Troops

Focusing on one troop type makes you vulnerable. Maintain a balanced army.

3. Wasting Speedups

Using speedups outside of events wastes potential extra rewards.

Pro Tip

Always keep your resources below your warehouse protection limit when offline to prevent losing them in attacks.
