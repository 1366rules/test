PvP Attack Fundamentals

Proper troop layering is the difference between crushing victories and devastating losses in Evony PvP. This guide covers advanced layering techniques for Keep Level 25+ players.

Key Concepts

  • Layers: T1-T9 troops that absorb hits before your main T10-T13
  • March Capacity: Fill 70% with layers, 30% with main attack force
  • Counter System: Infantry > Cavalry > Archers > Infantry
  • Power Ratio: Never attack players >20% stronger than you

Basic Layering Structure

Every successful attack march should contain these essential components:

Layer Type Troop Composition Purpose Recommended Amount
Meat Shield T1-T3 Infantry Absorb initial hits 10,000-20,000
Counter Layer T4-T6 of counter type Engage enemy main force 5,000-10,000
Main Attack T10-T12 of counter type Deal primary damage Fill remaining capacity
Siege T9+ Siege Destroy traps/walls 500-1,000

Archer Bomb Attack

Devastating against infantry-heavy defenses but vulnerable to cavalry:

Optimal Layers
  • 20k T1 Infantry
  • 10k T2 Infantry
  • 5k T3 Infantry
  • 8k T4 Archers
  • 5k T5 Archers
Main Force
  • 150k T12 Archers
  • 30k T11 Archers
  • 1k T10 Siege
Best Generals
  • Electra (Archer Attack +35%)
  • Hua Mulan (Archer HP +25%)
  • Zhang Fei (Infantry Debuff)

When to Use Archer Bomb

  • Enemy has 60%+ infantry in scout report
  • Attacking siege-heavy keeps
  • During "Archer Attack" civilization buff
  • Against players with weak wall defenses

Cavalry Smash Attack

Blitzkrieg-style attack against archer-heavy defenses:

Layer Type Troops Purpose
Shock Absorbers 30k T1-T3 Cavalry Trigger traps first
Flank Protectors 15k T4-T6 Infantry Counter enemy cavalry
Main Cavalry 200k T11-T12 Cavalry Crush archers
Cleanup Crew 5k T10 Siege Destroy remaining defenses

Cavalry Attack Buff Priority

  1. March Speed (Arrive before reinforcements)
  2. Cavalry Attack (Minimum +50% total)
  3. Enemy Archer Debuff (General skills)
  4. March Size (More troops = more damage)

Rally Attack Strategies

Coordinate with your alliance to take down powerful enemies:

Rally Composition
  • 50% Main Attack Type
  • 30% Counter Layer
  • 15% Meat Shields
  • 5% Siege
Timing Strategy
  • Launch when target is offline
  • Set rally to land during server quiet hours
  • Coordinate with alliance buffs
Advanced Tactics
  • Send fake rallies first
  • Combine with solo attacks
  • Use debuff generals first

Rally Target Selection

  • Power: 1.5x your rally power maximum
  • Check for active shield
  • Verify no alliance reinforcements
  • Confirm troop types with multiple scouts

Recognizing Attack Layers

Identify incoming attack types to prepare proper defenses:

Attack Signature Likely Composition Best Counter
Slow march speed (≥1:30) Infantry-heavy Archers + Traps
Fast march speed (≤0:45) Cavalry-heavy Pikemen + Ground
Medium speed (~1:00) Balanced/Archer Cavalry + Siege
Multiple similar marches Rally forming Shield or reinforce
